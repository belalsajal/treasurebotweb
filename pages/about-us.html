About TreasureBot

Our story, mission, and the team behind the green revolution

Who We Are

TreasureBot is a sustainability technology company founded in Berlin, Germany with a clear purpose: to revolutionize recycling through AI-powered technology and meaningful incentives. We've created a system that makes proper waste sorting effortless, rewards sustainable actions, and provides valuable data to improve recycling infrastructure.

Our Mission

To accelerate the world's transition to a circular economy by making sustainable choices simple, rewarding, and accessible to everyone. We aim to divert 10 million tons of waste from landfills by 2030 while creating measurable environmental impact through technology and behavioral science.

Our Vision

A world where waste is treated as a valuable resource, where recycling is effortless and rewarding, and where data-driven solutions optimize resource management at every level. We envision TreasureBot systems in every urban center, educational institution, and commercial facility worldwide, creating a global network of smart recycling.

Our Journey

The story of how TreasureBot evolved from academic research to a transformative sustainability solution

2022

The Concept Takes Shape

The concept of TreasureBot was born from a deep exploration of sustainable development and clean technologies during a master's thesis. This research involved studying global waste infrastructures, circular economy practices, and regulatory frameworks, combining empirical data analysis with firsthand insights from around the world. The findings highlighted the immense potential for technology to dramatically improve recycling efficiency.

2023

From Vision to Development

Recognizing the opportunity to address critical waste management challenges, we began developing skills in data science and artificial intelligence, envisioning an autonomous, AI-driven waste bin that could address inefficiencies, reduce contamination, and foster a circular economy.

2024

Collaboration & Refinement

Our involvement with Le Wagon further refined this idea, providing access to advanced AI tools and experts in computer vision, deep learning, and environmental science. This collaboration helped transform academic insights into practical technological solutions aligned with real-world needs.

2025

Transformative Solution Launch

TreasureBot now represents a powerful fusion of academic insights and real-world innovation, designed to boost recycling rates and align with the EU's ambitious environmental targets. By merging sustainability principles with cutting-edge technology, TreasureBot embodies a transformative solution to pressing waste management challenges, paving the way for a more sustainable future.
